About Us

Welcome to Purnea Public School — Shaping Minds, Building Futures.

Purnea Public School is a leading educational institution committed to nurturing young minds with academic excellence, moral values, and 21st-century skills. Located in the heart of Purnea, our school provides a safe, inclusive, and intellectually stimulating environment for students from Nursery to Class 12.

With a team of experienced teachers, smart classrooms, and modern facilities, we focus on the holistic development of every child — empowering them to become confident, responsible, and compassionate global citizens.

Join us in our mission to inspire learning, ignite curiosity, and unlock every student’s full potential.
